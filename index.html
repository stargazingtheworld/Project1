Pet Care Tips

For Dogs

Regular exercise, a balanced diet, and routine vet check-ups are essential. Make sure to provide mental stimulation with toys and training.

For Cats

Keep their litter clean, provide scratching posts, and engage them with interactive toys. Cats enjoy high spots, so consider cat trees or shelves.

For Rabbits

Rabbits need space to hop and play, along with a diet rich in hay and fresh greens. Provide safe chew toys to keep their teeth healthy.
